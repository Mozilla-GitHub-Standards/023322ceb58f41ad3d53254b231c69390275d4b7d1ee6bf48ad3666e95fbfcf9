Mozilla Presence Service

Overview

Welcome to the Mozilla Presence Service.

The Mozilla Presence Service is a new experimental pilot project to provide a presence feature on Firefox OS. It is currently in its early stages, but already provides a basic service experience.

To try out the service, hit the buttons below on your Firefox OS device or on Firefox Desktop.

If you want to learn more & contribute, please check our wiki page.
